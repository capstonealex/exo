Represents a rectangular matrix with a banded storage. 


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}

\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Rows} & Number of rows, or {\bfseries{Dynamic}} \\
\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Cols} & Number of columns, or {\bfseries{Dynamic}} \\
\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Supers} & Number of super diagonal \\
\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Subs} & Number of sub diagonal \\
\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Options} & A combination of either {\bfseries{\mbox{\hyperlink{group__enums_ggaacded1a18ae58b0f554751f6cdf9eb13a77c993a8d9f6efe5c1159fb2ab07dd4f}{Row\+Major}}}} or {\bfseries{\mbox{\hyperlink{group__enums_ggaacded1a18ae58b0f554751f6cdf9eb13a0103672ae41005ab03b4176c765afd62}{Col\+Major}}}}, and of {\bfseries{\mbox{\hyperlink{group__enums_gga39e3366ff5554d731e7dc8bb642f83cdacf9ccb2016f8b9c0f3268f05a1e75821}{Self\+Adjoint}}}} The former controls storage order, and defaults to column-\/major. The latter controls whether the matrix represents a selfadjoint matrix in which case either Supers of Subs have to be null.\\
